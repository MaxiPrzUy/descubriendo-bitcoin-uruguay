# Pre-event Survey

This survey is used at the beginning of each workshop to understand the participant profile, previous knowledge, interests, and learning needs.

## Suggested questions

1. Name and surname (optional).
2. City / department.
3. Age range.
4. Occupation or sector.
5. Is this your first time attending a Bitcoin activity?
6. Before this workshop, how much did you feel you knew about Bitcoin? Scale from 1 to 5.
7. What are you most interested in learning today?
8. How did you hear about this workshop?
9. What question would you like this workshop to answer?

## Purpose

The goal is to measure the starting point of each audience and compare it with the post-event survey.

## Privacy

All personal information should be optional and collected only with participant consent.

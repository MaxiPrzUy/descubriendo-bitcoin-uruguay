# KPI Framework

Descubriendo Bitcoin Uruguay measures success through quantitative and qualitative indicators.

## Short-term KPIs

- Complete 6 free in-person Bitcoin education workshops.
- Reach at least 420 in-person participants.
- Achieve an average of 70 attendees per workshop.
- Achieve at least 600 registrations through Luma or institutional forms.
- Measure attendance through Luma check-ins, institutional lists, entry forms, or on-site counts.
- Have at least 300 opt-in participants join the educational database, WhatsApp groups, or community channels.
- Collect at least 250 pre-event surveys.
- Collect at least 250 post-event surveys.
- Measure at least a 25% improvement in understanding of Bitcoin, self-custody, financial freedom, digital sovereignty, security, and responsible use.
- Achieve an average satisfaction score of at least 4/5.
- Achieve a positive NPS.
- Involve at least 3 institutions, universities, educational spaces, or local partners.
- Reach at least 100,000 views or people reached on social media.
- Publish at least 24 educational content pieces.
- Identify at least 10 potential partners, speakers, institutions, or community leaders for future editions.

## Long-term indicators

- Repeat participation.
- Continued activity in community groups.
- New institutions interested in future editions.
- Quality of educational materials produced.
- Usefulness of the organizer toolkit.
- Communities requesting or adopting the methodology.
- Growth of the community before Bitcoin Week Uruguay 2027.
- Potential replication in Uruguay, Argentina, and Brazil.

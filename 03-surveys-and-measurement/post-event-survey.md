# Post-event Survey

This survey is used at the end of each workshop to measure learning, satisfaction, community interest, and future educational needs.

## Suggested questions

1. After the workshop, how much do you feel you understand Bitcoin? Scale from 1 to 5.
2. Did the workshop help you understand Bitcoin better?
3. How satisfied were you with the activity? Scale from 1 to 10.
4. Would you recommend this workshop to another person? Scale from 0 to 10.
5. What was the most valuable thing you learned?
6. What would you improve for future editions?
7. What topic would you like to explore further?
8. Would you like to participate in future workshops?
9. Would you like to join the community and receive future educational content?
10. Would you like to collaborate as a volunteer, partner, or local reference?

## Consent

All participant data must be collected voluntarily and used only for educational follow-up and impact measurement.

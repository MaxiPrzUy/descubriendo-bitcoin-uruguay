# Roadmap 2026–2028

## 2026 — Pilot stage

The first stage of Descubriendo Bitcoin Uruguay will be developed in Maldonado, Uruguay.

### Goals

- Run 6 free in-person Bitcoin education workshops.
- Reach at least 420 in-person participants.
- Achieve at least 600 registrations through Luma or institutional forms.
- Build a voluntary educational database of at least 300 people.
- Measure learning improvement through pre- and post-event surveys.
- Reach at least 100,000 people through educational content and social media distribution.
- Publish at least 24 educational or documentary content pieces.
- Work with at least 3 institutions or local partners.
- Identify at least 10 potential partners, speakers, or community leaders.
- Build a stronger community foundation for Bitcoin Week Uruguay 2027.

## 2027 — Expansion stage

After validating the pilot, the goal is to expand the program to other departments of Uruguay.

### Goals

- Reach 8 to 10 departments.
- Work with universities, local institutions, communities, and educational spaces.
- Identify local leaders and future speakers.
- Improve the methodology and materials based on the 2026 pilot.
- Strengthen Bitcoin Week Uruguay 2027 with a larger and better-informed community.

## 2028 — Replication stage

The long-term goal is to consolidate an open and replicable Bitcoin education model for Uruguay and Spanish-speaking communities in Latin America.

# Contributing

Descubriendo Bitcoin Uruguay is an open Bitcoin education initiative.

We welcome suggestions, improvements, translations, adaptations, and feedback from educators, Bitcoin communities, institutions, and local organizers.

## How to contribute

You can contribute by:

- Suggesting improvements to the educational materials.
- Translating materials into other languages.
- Adapting the methodology for your local community.
- Sharing feedback after using the materials.
- Proposing new workshop formats.
- Improving surveys or impact measurement tools.
- Sharing local use cases or examples.

## Principles

Contributions should respect the following principles:

- Bitcoin-only focus.
- Non-speculative education.
- No trading or investment advice.
- Clear language for beginners.
- Respect for participant privacy and consent.
- Open knowledge and community learning.

## Contact

If you use or adapt these materials, we would be happy to hear from you.

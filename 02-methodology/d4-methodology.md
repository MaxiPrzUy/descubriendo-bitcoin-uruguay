# D4 Methodology

The D4 methodology is the educational framework used by Descubriendo Bitcoin Uruguay.

It is designed to help people move from curiosity to understanding, from understanding to real-world examples, and from there to community participation.

## 1. Discover

Introduce what Bitcoin is, why it was created, and what problem it solves.

The goal is not to overwhelm participants with technical details, but to open a first door of understanding.

## 2. Debate

Create a space for questions, myths, doubts, and collective reflection.

This turns the workshop into a participatory experience instead of a one-way lecture.

## 3. Demonstrate

Present real-world use cases and invited speakers from different sectors.

Depending on the audience, speakers may include merchants, developers, educators, tourism operators, entrepreneurs, or members of Bitcoin communities.

## 4. Do the next step

Invite participants to continue learning, access educational materials, join the community, and participate in future activities.

## Summary

Curiosity → Understanding → Real-world cases → Community

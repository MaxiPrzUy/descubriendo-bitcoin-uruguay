# Descubriendo Bitcoin Uruguay

**Descubriendo Bitcoin Uruguay** is an open, measurable, and replicable Bitcoin education and onboarding program for Spanish-speaking communities, piloted in Uruguay.

## Purpose

The project aims to introduce new audiences to Bitcoin through clear, responsible, non-speculative education.

It is not a trading program, investment course, or financial advice initiative. Its focus is Bitcoin education, self-custody, financial freedom, digital sovereignty, open knowledge, and community.

## Pilot

The first stage will be developed in Maldonado, Uruguay, through six free in-person workshops during 2026.

The goal is to test the methodology, measure impact, document learnings, and create materials that can later be used by other communities, educators, and institutions.

## Methodology: D4

Each workshop follows the D4 framework:

1. **Discover** — understand what Bitcoin is, why it was created, and what problem it solves.
2. **Debate** — open space for questions, myths, doubts, and collective reflection.
3. **Demonstrate** — present real-world use cases and invited speakers.
4. **Do the next step** — invite participants to continue learning and join the community.

## 2026 KPIs

- 6 free in-person workshops.
- At least 420 in-person participants.
- At least 600 registrations through Luma or institutional forms.
- At least 300 opt-in participants in the educational database or community channels.
- At least 250 pre-event surveys and 250 post-event surveys.
- At least 25% improvement in Bitcoin understanding.
- At least 100,000 views or people reached through educational content.
- At least 24 educational or documentary content pieces.
- At least 3 institutions or local partners involved.
- At least 10 potential partners, speakers, or community leaders identified.

## Open education model

This is not an open-source software project, but it is designed with an open-source mindset.

The materials, methodology, surveys, workshop structure, and organizer toolkit are intended to be reused, adapted, and improved by other communities that want to educate new audiences about Bitcoin.
